Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our supercar rental services.

How do I book a supercar? +
Simply browse our fleet, select your desired car, click "Book Now," fill in your details (name, phone, email, delivery address, and rental duration), and proceed to payment. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after booking.
What is the minimum rental period? +
The minimum rental period is 1 day (24 hours). You can also rent by the week or month for better rates.
Can I extend my rental period? +
Yes! Contact us at least 24 hours before your rental ends to extend. Extensions are subject to vehicle availability and will be charged at the same daily rate.
What documents do I need to rent a car? +
You need a valid driving license from your country of origin, an International Driving Permit (if required), and a valid passport or Emirates ID. The minimum age is 21 years.
Can I cancel my booking? +
Yes. Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before delivery. 50% refund for 24-48 hours. No refund for cancellations less than 24 hours before delivery.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and USDT (Tether) via the TRC20 network. All prices are in USD.
Is a security deposit required? +
No! Unlike most rental companies, we do not require any security deposit. You only pay the rental rate for the number of days you book.
How does USDT (crypto) payment work? +
Select USDT as your payment method, scan the QR code or copy our wallet address, and send the exact amount via the TRC20 network. Your booking will be confirmed once the transaction is verified on the blockchain.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay. Delivery is free. Additional charges only apply for extra kilometers beyond 250 km/day (AED 5/km) or traffic fines.
Do you deliver the car to my location? +
Yes! We offer free doorstep delivery anywhere in Dubai. Simply provide your hotel, apartment, or any address during booking.
How long does delivery take? +
We deliver at your scheduled time. We recommend booking at least 2 hours in advance for same-day delivery. A 30-minute grace period applies.
Can I pick up the car myself? +
Yes, you can pick up the car from our location in Dubai. Let us know your preference during booking.
Is the car insured? +
Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. In case of damage, a deductible of AED 3,000 applies.
What happens if I get into an accident? +
Immediately contact the police and obtain a police report. Then notify us within 24 hours. Our insurance will cover the damage minus the deductible amount.
Am I responsible for traffic fines? +
Yes, any traffic fines or violations incurred during your rental period are your responsibility. We will notify you and provide the details.
What is the mileage limit? +
Standard mileage is 250 km per day. Additional kilometers are charged at AED 5 per km.
Can I drive the car outside Dubai? +
Yes, you can drive anywhere within the UAE. Driving outside the UAE is not permitted. Off-road driving is strictly prohibited.
Why are your prices in USD? +
We price in USD for international convenience and to simplify crypto payments. The USD is pegged to the AED, so the value is stable.
